About PyData

An Educational Program by NumFOCUS

PyData provides a forum for the international community of users and developers of data analysis tools to share ideas and learn from each other. We promote discussion of best practices, new approaches, and emerging technologies for data management, processing, analytics, and visualization.

Our community works with many languages including Python, Julia, and R. We're organized by NumFOCUS, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, and all proceeds benefit open-source development.
