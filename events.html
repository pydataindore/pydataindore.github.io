Event Guidelines

To ensure a great experience for everyone, please note:

  • All events follow our Code of Conduct
  • Registration is required for all events
  • Arrive 10-15 minutes early for in-person events
  • Bring your laptop for hands-on workshops
  • Recordings are shared on our YouTube channel
  • Network and connect during breaks!
