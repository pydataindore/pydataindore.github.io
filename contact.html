FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are events free to attend?

Most of our regular meetups and workshops are free. Our annual conference may have a nominal fee to cover venue and catering costs, with scholarships available for those who need financial assistance.

Do I need to be an expert to attend?

Absolutely not! We welcome everyone from complete beginners to seasoned experts. Our events are designed to be inclusive and educational for all skill levels.

How can I propose a talk?

Use the contact form above with "Speaker Proposal" as the subject, or email us directly. Include your talk title, abstract, and a brief bio. We provide mentorship for first-time speakers!

Can my company host a PyData meetup?

Yes! We're always looking for venue partners. Hosting a meetup is a great way to showcase your office and connect with the data community. Contact us to discuss.

How do I become a volunteer?

Fill out the contact form with "Volunteer Opportunity" as the subject, telling us about yourself and how you'd like to help. We'll reach out to discuss next steps.
